HousingHub Policies

Our policies exist to protect tenants, landlords, brokers, and staff. Read through them carefully to understand your rights and responsibilities on the platform.

👨‍🏠

Tenant Responsibilities

  • Timely payment of rent and utilities as agreed in the lease
  • Maintaining property cleanliness, hygiene, and safety at all times
  • Reporting maintenance issues promptly through the HousingHub platform
  • Respecting neighbours, building rules, and community standards
  • Not making unauthorized alterations, modifications, or damage to the property
  • Giving proper notice before vacating as specified in the lease agreement
  • Allowing authorized property inspections with reasonable notice
🏠

Property Owner & Broker Responsibilities

  • Providing accurate, truthful property listings with no misleading information
  • Ensuring all properties meet safety, health, and legal housing standards
  • Responding promptly to tenant inquiries, applications, and maintenance requests
  • Respecting tenant privacy and providing proper notice before property visits
  • Following fair housing practices without discrimination of any kind
  • Maintaining clear lease agreements with transparent and honest rental terms
  • Keeping proper records of payments, contracts, and tenant communications
  • Brokers must act professionally and avoid unauthorized fees or fraudulent activity
💰

Broker Commission Rules

  • Brokers earn commissions that are clearly stated and agreed upon in writing
  • Commission fees are transparent and only disclosed after tenant payments are confirmed
  • No broker is permitted to collect hidden, unauthorized, or inflated charges
  • All commission payments must be documented through official HousingHub receipts
  • Brokers must comply with local laws and regulations governing agency and rental commissions
  • Any disputes regarding commission amounts or timing will be reviewed by HousingHub management
  • Repeated commission violations may result in suspension or permanent removal from the platform
🔍

Property Inspection Rules

  • All property inspections must be scheduled and agreed upon with tenants in advance
  • Owners and brokers must provide at least 24–48 hours notice before any visit
  • Emergency inspections are only permitted when urgent safety or structural risks exist
  • Inspections must respect tenant privacy and cause minimal disruption to daily life
  • Inspection findings, reports, and photos must be recorded and shared via HousingHub
  • Tenants may report improper, unannounced, or harassing inspections to HousingHub support
  • Move-in and move-out inspection checklists must be completed and signed by both parties
📋

Tenancy & Rent Agreement Policy

  • A user becomes an official tenant only after a successful and verified payment on HousingHub
  • Tenancy records are created automatically in the system upon payment confirmation
  • Each tenancy is linked to a specific property, payment reference, and action (rent/buy/lease)
  • Active tenants have access to their lease, payment history, and maintenance portal
  • Tenancy status may be set to active, expired, or terminated by the landlord or admin
  • Tenants must be formally offboarded when vacating, with all outstanding balances cleared
  • Sub-letting or transferring tenancy without landlord approval is strictly prohibited
👔

Employment & Staff Conduct Policies

  • All HousingHub staff must maintain professionalism when dealing with tenants and property owners
  • Employees must protect tenant and landlord data and honour all confidentiality agreements
  • Staff must provide timely, accurate, and respectful support to all platform users
  • Unethical behaviour, corruption, bribery, or harassment is strictly prohibited and actionable
  • All employees must follow company procedures for complaints and dispute resolution
  • System administrators must ensure platform security and enforce proper access control
  • Staff are expected to communicate openly, respectfully, and transparently at all times
⚖️

Termination & Staff Discipline Procedures

  • All HousingHub staff must comply with professional, ethical, and legal standards
  • Policy violations may result in formal warnings, suspension, or contract termination
  • Serious misconduct such as fraud, harassment, or data misuse leads to immediate dismissal
  • Every employee has the right to a fair and documented investigation before any disciplinary action
  • Repeated negligence, poor performance, or customer complaints may result in contract termination
  • All disciplinary actions are formally documented and reviewed by senior management
  • Departing staff must return all company credentials, devices, and access rights immediately
💲

Payment & Fees

  • Rent is due on the first day of each month unless otherwise specified in the lease
  • Late payments may incur penalty fees as clearly outlined in the signed lease agreement
  • Security deposits are fully refundable upon lease completion subject to satisfactory inspection
  • Utility bills (water, electricity, internet) are the tenant’s responsibility unless stated otherwise
  • All payment receipts are issued and stored digitally through the HousingHub platform
  • Payments may be made via MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, debit/credit card, or bank transfer
  • Disputes on payments must be raised within 7 days of the transaction for review
🔧

Maintenance & Repairs

  • All maintenance requests must be submitted through the HousingHub maintenance portal
  • Emergency repairs such as electrical faults and burst pipes will be prioritised and addressed within 24 hours
  • Non-emergency requests will be scheduled within 72 hours based on technician availability
  • Tenants are responsible for minor upkeep and must report damages promptly to avoid escalation
  • Property managers must document all repair progress, costs, and completion status on the platform
  • Repairs caused by tenant negligence or misuse may be charged to the tenant’s account
  • Tenants can track the live status of their maintenance requests from their dashboard
🔒

Privacy & Data Protection

  • All tenant, landlord, and broker information is securely stored using encryption
  • Personal data is only shared with authorised personnel or when required by law
  • HousingHub fully complies with local and applicable data protection regulations
  • Tenant documents, lease agreements, and payment records are kept strictly confidential
  • Access logs and audit trails are maintained for security and accountability purposes
  • Users may request deletion or amendment of their personal data by contacting support
  • HousingHub will never sell or share user data with third parties for commercial purposes
⚖️

Legal & Compliance

  • All lease agreements and property contracts comply with Ugandan housing and tenancy laws
  • Evictions are only carried out following proper legal procedures and adequate notice periods
  • Security deposit handling adheres strictly to local housing and financial regulations
  • Non-compliance with platform rules may result in account suspension or legal referral
  • HousingHub reserves the right to remove listings or accounts that violate legal standards
  • Disputes that cannot be resolved on the platform may be referred to relevant Ugandan authorities
💬

Feedback & Complaint Handling

  • Tenants, landlords, and brokers may submit complaints anonymously or with identification
  • All complaints are logged, tracked, and followed up until full resolution
  • Response times and follow-ups are actively monitored through the HousingHub platform
  • Feedback surveys are issued regularly to all users to improve platform quality and service
  • Management will communicate all resolutions transparently to the parties involved
  • Unresolved or escalated complaints may be reviewed by senior HousingHub management
  • Malicious, false, or abusive complaints may result in account review or suspension
